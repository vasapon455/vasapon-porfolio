About Me

Vasapon Opaswatanakul (also known as Bright, born December 30, 1996) is a writer, who has been working in the online content industry for over three years. He has had a passion for writing since high school. At that time he really love writing and reading the books. he also wrote some poems and post them on his Facebook. Getting a lot of inspiration and feedback from his acquaintances, he decided to continue his studies in the field of content writing.

After spending four years in the Faculty of Communication Arts at Chulalongkorn University, he discovered that he wanted to become a professional content writer. After graduating from the university, he applied for his first job in journalism at the media organization known as “the MATTER”.

Until now, he has gained extensive working experience with famous media organizations such as The STANDARD POP, The MATTER, UNLOCKMEN, and more. He is currently employed as a UX Writer at KTC, where he is involved in various projects including KTC Mobile, MAAI, and KTC.co.th.
